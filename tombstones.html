Tombstones

Stone Panels Slab Stones Urn / Pillow Stones Plant Stones Edges / Enclosures

Stone Panels

Available in vertical or horizontal format, with a maximum face area of 1.5 m².

Slab Stones

Natural stones with various finishes. Common dimensions: Length: 1.30 m x Width: 0.50 m x Height: 0.16/0.18 m.

Urn / Pillow Stones

Upright urn stones, also available in height 1.00 m x width 0.40 m. Lying pillow stones: Dimensions L 0.60 m x W 0.40 m, thickness as required.

Plant Stones

In vertical or horizontal format, with a maximum surface area of 1.5 m².

Tombstones with Edges/Enclosures

Enclosures usually made of natural stone, matching the tombstone. More affordable options also available in artificial stone.
