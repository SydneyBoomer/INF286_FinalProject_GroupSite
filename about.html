About our Company:





Our family-owned master craftsman company, which has existed since 1884, advises you competently and individually on the purchase of a new tombstone, with new inscriptions for your deceased and with any repair or renovation orders.

We take over the approval procedures at all cemeteries in Munich and the surrounding area. Our exhibition space next door (Ungererstr. 116) shows you a variety of forms, materials and processing options.

One of our main focuses is the post-inscribed by a master's hand, be it chiseled and painted, laid out in lead, noble gold writings or applied bronze and aluminum inscriptions on already existing tombstones, which are always inspected beforehand to achieve the best possible result.

Furthermore, as a guild company, we carry out repairs, cleaning and renovations on tombstones, wooden / forging crosses and edgings professionally, as well as erecting and removal work. We are happy to advise you in the search for the right grave accessories (grave lanterns, holy water kettles, vases etc.)

We look forward to welcoming you in our office studio!
